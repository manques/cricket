Advantages:

1) Match is over in about 3 overs. Since time is off essence in our fast paced lives, most people prefer a shorter game.

2) More entertainment than ODI or Test match cricket in a short burst.

3) It closes the gap between the good teams and the lesser teams in that it provides the lesser team with a better chance to pull off an upset.

Disadvantages:

1) Quality of cricket is compromised as is the technique of players. Their temperament is not put to the test. Its a slog fest for most players.

2) It doesn't provide the enthralling drama that we see in test cricket over a long period of time.

3) It discourages the bowlers by shortening the boundary and putting in fielding restrictions. T20 is a batsman's game.
